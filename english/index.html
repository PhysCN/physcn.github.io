Introduction

The research group of photoelectric functional materials in Henan University of Science and Technology was founded by ProfessorLiben Li in 2005. There are about the twenty faculty members in our team. The current team leader is Dr. Zheng-Wei Zuo. The team focuses on the research of dielectric materials such as ferroelectric material, search of excellent materials by first-principles calculation methods, topological materials, machine learning, etc. Most members have solid scientific research background, and are good at analyze and solve frontier scientific issues. Energyone is highly innovative and creative. In recent years, the team has obtained more than 15 projects of National Natural Science Foundation of China and published more than 50 high-level papers. More than 40 patents have been applied, including more than 20 granted invention patents.
